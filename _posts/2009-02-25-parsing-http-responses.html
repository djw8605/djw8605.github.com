--- title: Parsing HTTP Responses date: '2009-02-25T14:56:00.001-06:00' author: Derek Weitzel tags: - Network - Class modified_time: '2009-02-25T14:59:38.181-06:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3007054864987759910.post-7919870733930834351 blogger_orig_url: http://derekweitzel.blogspot.com/2009/02/parsing-http-responses.html --- Just so I don't have to remember again, in order to seperate a header from the body of a http response, in C:

char* endOfHeader = strstr(buf, "\n\r\n");

Notice the \r, that is a carriage return, something that Linux/unix doesn't care much about, but is used more in Windows. I couldn't find any websites to help with this.
