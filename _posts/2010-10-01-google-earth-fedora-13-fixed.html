--- title: Google Earth Fedora 13 Fixed! date: '2010-10-01T16:50:00.001-05:00' author: Derek Weitzel tags: - nvidia - X modified_time: '2010-10-03T00:48:13.850-05:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3007054864987759910.post-3346827088283685962 blogger_orig_url: http://derekweitzel.blogspot.com/2010/10/google-earth-fedora-13-fixed.html --- I was able to fix the install of google earth on fedora 13 by installing the i686 nvidia libraries.

After adding rpm forge and installing the nvidia drivers (kmod-nvidia). It only installed the x86_64 nvidia libs.

yum install xorg-x11-drv-nvidia-libs.i686


Working!!!
