[GP-ENGINE](https://gp-engine.org) expands advanced computing adoption in the Great Plains region through coordinated GPU-enabled research infrastructure.

The project is designed to increase researcher and student access to local and national resources while strengthening campus research computing capacity.

My work supports engineering and operations patterns that help institutions participate effectively in shared, distributed GPU and data workflows.

I am a Co-PI on GP-ENGINE.
