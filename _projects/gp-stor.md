[GP-STOR](https://gp-stor.org) is a distributed storage initiative for the Great Plains region focused on resilient, tiered object storage for research workloads.

The platform emphasizes practical access for researchers while maintaining multi-site durability and operational resilience.

My project focus includes service architecture, integration with research computing environments, and reliable data access patterns for scientific users.

I am the PI for GP-STOR.
